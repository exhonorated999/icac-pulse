Core Capabilities

Nine reasons P.U.L.S.E. is different.

01

Sovereignty Over Your Data

P.U.L.S.E. operates with a local-first architecture — 100% of your data stays offline, stored on your machine or external drive. With built-in AES-256 encryption, your case files remain under your control and off the grid.

Security

Mission-Critical Dashboard

Command your caseload from a centralized hub. Track open, closed, and transferred cases alongside arrest metrics and warrant counts. Smart Alerts trigger the moment a warrant production falls past due — so you never miss a deadline.
